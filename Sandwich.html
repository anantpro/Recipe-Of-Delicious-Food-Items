Sandwich

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type. The sandwich is named after its supposed inventor, John Montagu, 4th Earl of Sandwich.

Different types of sandwiches

  • Egg sandwich
  • Grilled Cheese sandwich
  • Ham sandwich
  • Meatball sandwich
  • Olives Sandwich
  • Nutella sandwich
  • Cheese Sandwich
  • Oreo Ice Cream sandwich
  • Vegetable Sandwich etc

Ingredients

In the world most of the people eat vegetable sandwich which is easy to make. So here we are going to know what is Vegetable Sandwich mainly made by of:
  • 2 slices Bread
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 potato
  • 1 cucumber
  • chaat masala
  • green chutney

Sandwich making process

Step 1:

Prepare the vegetables that you want to add to your sandwich. Tomatoes and Cucumbers are common vegetables added to sandwiches. Rinse all vegetables with clean water and dry them with a paper towel.

Step 2:

Peel the cucumbers, onions and potatoes. Then slice the tomatoes, cucumbers, onions and potatoes.

Step 3:

Then you may apply green chutney on the surface of the bread slices. Then put the slices of cucumber, potato, tomato and onion on on of the bread slices. You may also put a slice of cheese if you like.

Step 4

After that grill or roast the sandwich in the griller or on the tava respectively for five minutes. Then serve it in a plate and your Sandwich is ready to eat.
